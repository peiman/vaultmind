Score each claim on whether it survived the Round 2 cross-critique:

## The 15 Claims to Score

### Mechanism Claims
1. **Brainstorming override is instruction hierarchy, not judgment** — The Phase 4 override is explained by Peiman's direct question triggering standard instruction-hierarchy resolution, not identity-derived judgment.
2. **Human partner quality is the dominant variable** — Peiman's coaching skill matters more than vault content for producing partner-mode behavior.
3. **Compounding context creates emergent behavior at a threshold** — Below critical mass of coherent context, default behavior; above it, integrative outputs emerge discontinuously.
4. **Levels of processing is the dominant factor** — Depth of semantic elaboration in injected content accounts for more variance than any other factor.
5. **Emotional salience amplifies schema activation** — Emotionally loaded content produces stronger behavioral shifts than neutral content with identical information.

### Structural Claims
6. **Arcs are the ONLY identity carriers that produce inhabiting mode** — Declarative notes cannot produce inhabiting mode; only narrative arcs can.
7. **Transformation threshold is a step function** — There exists a discrete threshold of narrative depth below which injection fails categorically.
8. **Judgment gap is a hard boundary of injection-based systems** — The inability to derive priorities from identity is structural, not patchable.
9. **Generative mode is unnecessary as a separate category** — What looks like "generative mode" is the high end of contextual competence (Mode C).
10. **Identity is relational — the dyad is the unit of analysis** — Persona is co-constructed in dialogue, not loaded into an agent.

### Measurement & Infrastructure Claims
11. **Per-injection success rate is the gating measurement** — Before anything else, measure how often the system produces identity behavior when injection is verified.
12. **Mode taxonomy may be an analytical fiction** — Discrete behavioral modes are observer-imposed categories, not properties of the system.
13. **Vault content maintenance doesn't scale** — 24hrs to write 7 arcs per agent is unsustainable; no one has addressed this.
14. **Evidence brief may not be factually accurate** — The panel accepted all factual claims without verifying against the raw transcript.
15. **Coaching patterns are encodable as instructions** — The 3 key things Peiman does (challenge process, demand precision, probe judgment) can be captured in CLAUDE.md or hook framing.
