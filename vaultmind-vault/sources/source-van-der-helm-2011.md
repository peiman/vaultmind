Current Biology study demonstrating that REM sleep specifically reduces the amygdala's emotional response to previously encoded emotionally aversive stimuli. After a night of sleep — particularly after more REM — fMRI showed attenuated amygdala activity and reduced subjective emotional tone when recalling the images the next morning. This provided direct neural support for the "overnight therapy" hypothesis.
