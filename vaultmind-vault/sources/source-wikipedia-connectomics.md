# Wikipedia — Connectomics

Reference overview of connectomics covering both macroscale (MRI-based, e.g., Human Connectome Project) and microscale (EM-based reconstruction of individual cells and synapses) approaches. Discusses landmark organisms (C. elegans, Drosophila, mouse cortex), key initiatives, and applications to plasticity and disease. Used as the high-level orientation for the [[connectomics|Connectomics]] note when no single primary paper covers the breadth of the field.
