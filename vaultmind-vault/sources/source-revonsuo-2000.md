Revonsuo's 2000 Behavioral and Brain Sciences target article proposing that dreaming evolved as a threat-simulation system. The paper argues that the biological function of dreaming is to rehearse threat perception and avoidance, drawing on cross-cultural and clinical evidence for the prevalence of threatening dream content.
