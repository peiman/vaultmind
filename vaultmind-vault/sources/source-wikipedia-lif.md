## Wikipedia — Biological Neuron Model (LIF)

Reference overview covering the family of mathematical neuron models. The LIF section gives the governing equation `C_m dV/dt = I(t) − V(t)/R_m`, the RC-circuit interpretation (membrane capacitance + leak resistance), threshold-and-reset spiking dynamics, the f-I curve formula with refractory period, and adaptive variants that address LIF's lack of spike-frequency adaptation. Also covers neighbor models — Hodgkin-Huxley, FitzHugh-Nagumo, Izhikevich, AdEx, GLM — and their tradeoffs.
