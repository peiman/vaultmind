Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience study examining whether whole episodic memories are replayed in dreams. While 65% of dream reports contained elements recognisable from waking life, only 1–2% represented intact episodic replay of specific past events. Most incorporations were of emotional tone, characters, or semantic fragments — not narrative episodes. This supports a view of dreaming as memory recombination rather than replay.
