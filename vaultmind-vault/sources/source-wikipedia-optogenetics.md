# Wikipedia — Optogenetics

Reference overview of optogenetics: light-activated ion channels (channelrhodopsin variants), pumps (halorhodopsin, archaerhodopsin), and enzymes for cell-type-specific neural control. Covers the 2002–2005 foundational period (Nagel, Hegemann, Deisseroth, Boyden), Nature Methods' Method-of-the-Year designation in 2010, applications in fear, learning, decision-making, addiction, and emerging vision-restoration therapies. Used as the broad-context source for the [[optogenetics|Optogenetics]] note alongside the Boyden 2005 primary paper.
