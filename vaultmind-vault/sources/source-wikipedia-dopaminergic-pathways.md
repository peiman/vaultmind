# Wikipedia — Dopaminergic pathways

Reference article on the dopaminergic projection systems of the brain. Origins: substantia nigra pars compacta (SNc) and ventral tegmental area (VTA) in the midbrain. Major pathways:

- **Nigrostriatal** (SNc → dorsal striatum): motor control; degeneration causes Parkinson's disease.
- **Mesolimbic** (VTA → nucleus accumbens): reward processing, motivation, addiction.
- **Mesocortical** (VTA → prefrontal cortex): cognition, executive function.
- **Tuberoinfundibular** (hypothalamus → pituitary): inhibits prolactin release.

Article documents dopaminergic involvement in reward learning, movement, mood, and the link between SNc cell loss and Parkinsonian motor symptoms.
