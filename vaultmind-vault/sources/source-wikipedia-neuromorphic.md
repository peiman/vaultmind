## Wikipedia — Neuromorphic Engineering

Encyclopedic overview of neuromorphic engineering: Carver Mead's late-1980s framing, the lineage of analog VLSI neural circuits, the modern wave of digital and mixed-signal chips (TrueNorth, SpiNNaker, Neurogrid, Loihi, Loihi 2, Hala Point), memristor-based synapses, and the open ethical and IP questions raised by brain-inspired hardware. Used as a broad reference for the field-level concept note where pinning to a single primary citation would misrepresent the breadth.
