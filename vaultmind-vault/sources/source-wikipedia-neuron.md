# Wikipedia — Neuron

Canonical encyclopedic overview of the neuron as the fundamental signaling unit of the nervous system. Covers anatomy (soma, dendrites, axon, axon terminal, synapse), classification (sensory, motor, interneuron), basic electrophysiology, and the role of neurons in network-level computation.

Used as a primary reference for [[neuron|Neuron]] in VaultMind because the textbook-level neuroscience facts (cell-body/soma contains the nucleus, dendrites are the primary input surface, the axon carries the action potential, synapses are the contact point with downstream cells) are uncontested and well-cited within the article itself.
