# Wikipedia — Motor neuron

Reference article on motor neurons (motoneurons): efferent neurons whose axons innervate effectors (skeletal, smooth, or cardiac muscle, or glands). Distinguishes upper motor neurons (originating in motor cortex, including Betz cells, projecting via corticospinal/corticobulbar tracts) from lower motor neurons (originating in spinal ventral horn or brainstem motor nuclei, directly innervating muscle). Documents the alpha vs. gamma subtype distinction in somatic motor neurons (alpha innervate force-generating extrafusal fibers; gamma innervate intrafusal fibers within muscle spindles to set stretch sensitivity) and the neuromuscular junction as the cholinergic synapse where acetylcholine is released onto skeletal muscle.
