# Rudy et al. — Three groups of cortical interneurons (2011)

Influential review in *Developmental Neurobiology* establishing the now-canonical three-group taxonomy of neocortical GABAergic interneurons by molecular marker:

- **PV (parvalbumin)** ~40% of cortical interneurons; includes fast-spiking basket cells and chandelier cells; provide perisomatic and axo-axonic inhibition; critical for gamma oscillations.
- **SST (somatostatin)** ~30%; includes Martinotti cells; target distal dendrites of pyramidal neurons; gate dendritic integration.
- **5HT3aR** ~30%; heterogeneous group including all VIP+ neurons and neurogliaform cells; many target other interneurons (disinhibition).

Each group has a distinct embryological origin (medial vs. caudal ganglionic eminence) and corresponds to functionally and morphologically coherent classes. The framework underpins modern interneuron research and connectomics.
