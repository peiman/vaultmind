## Wikipedia — Long-Term Depression

Reference overview of LTD: definition as activity-dependent reduction in synaptic efficacy lasting hours or longer, role as the homeostatic counterpart to LTP that prevents synaptic saturation, and brain-region-specific mechanisms — hippocampal LTD (NMDAR / mGluR-dependent forms), cerebellar LTD at parallel-fiber-Purkinje synapses (the canonical motor-learning substrate), and variants in striatum, visual cortex, prefrontal cortex, and perirhinal cortex. Discusses LTD's emerging role in Alzheimer's pathology via soluble Aβ.
