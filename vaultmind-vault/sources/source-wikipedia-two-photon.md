# Wikipedia — Two-Photon Excitation Microscopy

Reference overview of two-photon microscopy: physical principle (simultaneous absorption of two longer-wavelength photons), history (Denk & Strickler, 1990, Cornell), advantages over single-photon confocal (depth, reduced photobleaching, suppressed background), and applications across neuroscience, cancer research, embryology and kidney research. Used as the orientation source for the [[two-photon-imaging|Two-Photon Calcium Imaging]] note.
