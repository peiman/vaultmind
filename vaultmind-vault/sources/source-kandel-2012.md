# Kandel et al. — Principles of Neural Science, 5th ed. (2012)

The standard graduate-level neuroscience textbook (McGraw-Hill, 2012; ISBN 978-0-07-139011-8). Eric Kandel and co-authors provide an integrated treatment of cellular and systems neuroscience: neuronal anatomy, ion channels and the Hodgkin-Huxley framework, synaptic transmission, neurotransmitter systems, sensory and motor systems, learning and memory.

Used as a textbook-level cross-reference for the neuron-biology concept cluster ([[neuron|Neuron]], [[action-potential|Action Potential]], [[synaptic-transmission|Synaptic Transmission]], [[neurotransmitters|Neurotransmitters]], [[ion-channels|Ion Channels]], [[membrane-potential|Membrane Potential]]). Kandel's own Nobel-winning work on synaptic plasticity in *Aplysia* makes the synaptic chapters particularly authoritative.
