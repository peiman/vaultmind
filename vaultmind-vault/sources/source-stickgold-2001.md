Comprehensive Science review linking sleep, learning, and dreaming under an offline memory reprocessing framework. The paper synthesised evidence that dream content preferentially incorporates recent waking experiences in fragmented, transformed form, and argued that this reprocessing serves adaptive memory consolidation rather than mere epiphenomenal replay.
