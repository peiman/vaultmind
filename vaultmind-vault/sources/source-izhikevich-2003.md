## Izhikevich — Simple Model of Spiking Neurons (2003)

Eugene M. Izhikevich's two-equation neuron model, published in *IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks* 14(6), 1569-1572. The model is famous for combining Hodgkin-Huxley-level richness with integrate-and-fire-level efficiency: just two ODEs and four parameters reproduce roughly twenty distinct cortical firing patterns (regular spiking, fast spiking, intrinsically bursting, chattering, low-threshold spiking, resonator, etc.). Tens of thousands of neurons can be simulated in real time on a desktop CPU. The model became a workhorse for large-scale spiking simulations and is a common building block in [[neuromorphic-computing|neuromorphic]] hardware research.
