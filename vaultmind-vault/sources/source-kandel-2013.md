# Kandel et al. — Principles of Neural Science, 5th edition (2013)

Canonical neuroscience textbook (McGraw-Hill, ISBN 978-0-07-139011-8), edited by Eric R. Kandel (Nobel laureate, 2000), James H. Schwartz, Thomas M. Jessell, Steven A. Siegelbaum, and A. J. Hudspeth. Comprehensive coverage of cellular neuroscience, systems neuroscience, and neurology. Used here as a textbook citation for canonical facts about neuron classifications, motor units, sensory transduction, basal ganglia / dopaminergic systems, and cerebellar circuitry. Chapters cited include those on the motor unit, spinal reflexes, sensory coding, somatosensory pathways, and brainstem motor/sensory nuclei.
