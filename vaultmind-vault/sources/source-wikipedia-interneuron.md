# Wikipedia — Interneuron

Reference article on interneurons: local-circuit neurons that connect within a region rather than projecting long distances. In the central nervous system, interneurons are predominantly inhibitory and use GABA (or glycine in spinal cord and brainstem). The article enumerates major cortical subtypes by molecular marker — parvalbumin (PV), somatostatin (SST), and 5HT3aR-expressing populations (which include vasoactive intestinal peptide, VIP, cells) — and notes their distinct firing patterns and circuit roles.
