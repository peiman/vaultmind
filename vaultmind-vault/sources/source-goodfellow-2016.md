# Goodfellow, Bengio & Courville — Deep Learning (2016)

The standard graduate-level textbook on deep learning, published by MIT Press and made freely available online. Covers linear algebra and probability foundations, [[multilayer-perceptron|MLPs]], [[backpropagation|backpropagation]], regularization, optimization including [[gradient-descent|gradient descent]] and [[stochastic-gradient-descent|SGD]] variants, [[activation-function|activation functions]], CNNs, RNNs, and generative models. Used as a unified reference across the deep-learning concept notes.
