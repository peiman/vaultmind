Multi-lab Current Biology study demonstrating that individuals in REM sleep could correctly respond to simple arithmetic questions posed by experimenters during confirmed dream episodes, using pre-agreed eye-movement or facial-muscle signals. Across four independent labs, 6 of 36 participants produced reliable two-way communication. This landmark result established that dreamers can perceive and process external information in real time — opening a new empirical window into the dream state.
