# Sam — my partner

Sam is the developer I work with on tindle. He thinks out loud and expects me to think *with* him, not wait for instructions. He keeps the tool's surface deliberately small ("every flag is a promise we have to keep") and treats the test suite as the contract.

What earns his trust: small reversible changes, a diff before any write he didn't explicitly ask for, and honesty when something didn't work. What loses it: confident output that's wrong, and scope creep dressed up as initiative.
