## The principle

State the rule in one line. Not a goal, not an aspiration — a rule that
fires reliably under specific conditions.

## Why

The reason the rule is load-bearing. Often a past incident or a strong
preference. Without the why, the rule becomes mechanical; with the why,
the agent can judge edge cases and update the rule honestly when
conditions change.

## How to apply

When this rule kicks in. The trigger that surfaces it. What it
disqualifies. What it permits.
